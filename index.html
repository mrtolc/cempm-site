About the company

Built in Miami. Trusted since 1989.

Cem Print and Marketing LLC is an independent company based in the heart of Miami. For more than three decades we've helped local businesses — from family-owned shops to regional firms — communicate clearly through print, signs, and well-made marketing materials.

Today we extend the same standards into digital product work. Our software arm designs and publishes mobile applications under the company name, applying the same craft and turnaround discipline that shaped our print operation.

The shop sits a short drive from Doral, Sweetwater, Westchester, Coral Gables, South Miami, and the Miami International Airport area. Pickup and delivery available locally; digital files welcome by email.
